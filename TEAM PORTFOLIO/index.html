Our Project

Online Health Monitoring and Barangay Management System

Our team proposed an Online Health Monitoring and Barangay Management System for Barangay Pasong Tamo, Quezon City. The Online Health Monitoring and Barangay Management System is a management information system that monitors the health of the barangay staff and citizens, and facilitates the work of barangay officers, personnel, and other workers. The ultimate goal of the Online Health Monitoring and Barangay Management System is to be automated to the point where all of the health, records and transactions that the barangay need are completely structured and accessible. By organizing the listing of workpapers, blotter reports, and requests from other persons, our system hopes to support the Barangay in their work. However, this project aims to implement a computerized health monitoring and management system for Barangay to determine the staff and citizen health issues and to lessen the use of drawers and log books. The automations of such repetitive tasks are what the researchers wish to resolve through the development of a management system.
