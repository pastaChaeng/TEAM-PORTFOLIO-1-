Previous Project

Design and Development of Exclusive Online Marketplace for Quezon City University

This research project is all about the Design and Development of Exclusive Marketplace for Quezon City University. It intends to create a system wherein its constituents are able to buy and sell goods to their fellow students and faculty. It aims to develop and boost the trading and small business inside the university by integrating innovative systems that occur online. Building a community that would band together with its constituents, especially under a certain discussion, is a good idea to develop and engage them with others. A system that would help students to form an online community with the main purpose of selling goods, whether second-hand or brand new items to their fellow students. The system will be called "STUDY AND SELL" which is an exclusive online buy and sell platform for the students of Quezon City University that would focus on listing items from students and facilitating transactions between users.
